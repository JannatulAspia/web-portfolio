Research Experience

Dual-Stage Vision Transformer for Alzheimer's Disease

Submitted as "Dual-Stage Vision Transformer (DS-ViT) with Attention-Guided Patch Aggregation for Alzheimer's Disease Classification" - International Conference 2026

Created a novel transformer-based global attention to classify four stages of Alzheimer's disease from MRI scans. Achieved 95.40% accuracy, outperforming state-of-the-art CNN and ViT models, demonstrating strong potential for early and automated dementia diagnosis.

Ensemble-ASNet for Kidney Stone Classification

Under Review as "Ensemble-ASNet: Weighted Transfer Learning Framework with Grad-CAM++ for Robust Kidney Stone Classification" - IEEE Conference 2025

Developed a weighted ensemble of InceptionV3, VGG16, and MobileNetV2 for accurate and interpretable kidney stone detection from CT scans. Integrated Grad-CAM++ for model interpretability and optimized ensemble weights for combining class probabilities. Achieved 99.81% accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score, validating clinical relevance and highlighting important regions in scans. Demonstrated a robust, precise, and explainable approach with potential for clinical deployment and multi-institutional dataset expansion.

Pavement Crack Detection using YOLOv9

Under Review as "Real-Time Pavement Crack Detection Using YOLOv9: A Deep Learning Approach for Infrastructure Monitoring" - IEEE Conference 2025

Built an automated system for real-time detection of four pavement crack types (pothole, longitudinal, transverse, alligator) using a custom-annotated dataset of 1,200 images. Trained and evaluated YOLOv9, achieving 73.2% F1-score and 70.4% mAP. The system provides precise, scalable, and rapid crack detection, supporting informed decision-making for road maintenance and infrastructure safety.

Smoker Status Prediction using Machine Learning

Accepted as "An Innovative Approach to Smoker Status Prediction with ML Algorithms" - International Conference 2025

Designed predictive models to classify smoking status of individuals using health and demographic features (age, height, weight, waist circumference, vision, hearing, blood pressure). Evaluated seven ML algorithms, including Na√Øve Bayes, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, Decision Tree, KNeighbors, and a novel HEStacked approach. The HEStacked model achieved 88.03% precision, demonstrating strong potential for public health interventions and targeted disease prevention.

Cross-Attention CNN-ViT Hybrid With Dual Explainability for Retinal OCT Classification

Manuscript in preparation for journal submission

Developed a hybrid CNN-ViT model leveraging cross-attention mechanisms to improve multi-class retinal OCT image classification. Integrated dual explainability techniques like Grad-Cam and Attention Map to visualize both spatial and feature-level attention, enhancing interpretability. Achieved high accuracy across 8 retinal disease classes, demonstrating a robust and explainable approach for ophthalmic diagnostics. Manuscript in preparation for journal submission.
